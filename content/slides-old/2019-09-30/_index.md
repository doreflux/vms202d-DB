## September 30, 2019

---

<br>
> One might simplify this by saying: _men act_ and _women appear_. Men look at women. Women watch themselves being looked at. This determines not only most relations between men and women but also the relation of women to themselves. The surveyor of woman in herself is male: the surveyed female. Thus she turns herself into an object &mdash; and most particularly an object of vision: a sight.

>&mdash; <cite>John Berger, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Ways of Seeing</span>, p. 47
